# Health-Assistant
Health Assistant is an AI-powered wellness chatbot that provides educational information and guidance on health-related topics. Users can ask questions in both English and Hindi, making health awareness more accessible and user-friendly.

Features

1.Supports English and Hindi languages.

2.AI-powered health chatbot.

3.Nutrition and healthy eating guidance.

4.Fitness and exercise recommendations.

5.Mental wellness tips.

6.Sleep hygiene suggestions.

7.Hydration awareness.

8.Basic first-aid information.

9.Daily health tips.

10.Chat history support.

11.Responsive and user-friendly interface.

12.Ask questions related to health, wellness, lifestyle, prevention, fitness, diet, and healthy habits.
